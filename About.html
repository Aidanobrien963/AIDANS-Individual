Who We Are

At Grub To Go, we take pride in crafting high-quality microwavable meals that are 100% locally produced right here in the heart of Ireland. Based in the scenic county of Cavan, our love for cooking has been the cornerstone of our journey, turning a passion for wholesome, delicious food into meals that bring convenience and flavor to your table. Each dish is carefully prepared using fresh, locally sourced ingredients to ensure every bite reflects the rich culinary heritage of Ireland. From traditional favorites to modern flavors, we’re dedicated to offering meals that not only taste great but also support our local community. We’re proud to be more than just a food brand – we’re part of the local fabric, providing over 50 people with jobs and contributing to the vibrant economy of Cavan.

Why Choose Us?

  • Locally sourced ingredients for better taste and nutrition.
  • Wide variety of healthy options, including vegan and low-calorie meals.
  • Available in convenient locations spread across the country: SuperValu, Centra and Londis.
